Our story

A centre built on care, sharpened by research.

Karunya Sugalaya is a dedicated Diabetes Care & Research Centre — purpose-built, not a department within a general hospital. Every protocol, every consultation room, every nurse is tuned to one thing: helping people live well, long-term, with diabetes.

We treat the whole person, not just a number on a glucometer. That means time spent listening, nutrition built around real Tamil kitchens, and follow-up that doesn't end at the clinic door.

Best-in-class control Structured plans that delay or prevent complications.
Compassionate practice Time, patience, and a doctor who remembers your name.
Modern technology CGM, insulin pumps, digital records — at Kumbakonam.
Community outreach Free diabetic camps & awareness drives across the region.

The Karunya approach

Four steps. One long relationship.

Diabetes care isn't a single visit — it's a partnership measured in years. Here's how we walk that road with you.
01

Listen & assess

A full clinical history, lifestyle screen and lab workup — before we suggest a single change.

02

Personalise

A plan built around your kitchen, work and budget. Medication, diet, movement — calibrated.

03

Equip & educate

Glucometers, CGM, pumps and the knowledge to use them. You leave knowing why, not just what.

04

Walk with you

Scheduled follow-ups, tele-consults, foot screenings and 24/7 support — for as long as you need.
