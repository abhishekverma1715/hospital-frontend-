Dr. K. Sivakumar Krishnamoorthy

MBBS, MD Founder & Managing Director

Dr. K. Sivakumar Krishnamoorthy is the visionary Founder and Managing Director of Karunya Sugalaya. His medical journey began with outstanding academic achievements, notably receiving the prestigious API Gold Medal during his early medical training, which set the foundation for a career defined by excellence and deep clinical inquiry.

Initially trained in general medicine, Dr. Sivakumar developed a profound interest in metabolic medicine, recognizing the growing epidemic of metabolic disorders. This shift in focus led him to dedicate his career to advancing the understanding and treatment of diabetes and related metabolic conditions, combining rigorous scientific research with compassionate patient care.

A true pioneer in health-tech integration, he is the architect behind DiaX.AI, an innovative artificial intelligence platform designed to predict and manage diabetic complications. Furthermore, he developed the Dialog EMR system, a comprehensive electronic medical record solution currently managing the complex health data of over 50,000 patients, ensuring precision and continuity in their care.

Dr. Sivakumar is deeply committed to advancing medical science through research. His ongoing studies and clinical trials are widely recognized, and he is scheduled to present his latest findings at the upcoming International Diabetes Federation (IDF) Congress in Bangkok in 2025, contributing to global conversations on metabolic health.

Beyond his clinical and research endeavors, he is a respected leader in the medical community. He currently serves as the Secretary of the Association of Physicians of India (API) Thanjavur chapter, where he plays a pivotal role in organizing educational programs and shaping local healthcare policies.

Dr. B. Lakshmi Sivakumar

MBBS, D.Diab Consultant & Director of Administration

Dr. B. Lakshmi Sivakumar is a highly skilled Consultant and the Director of Administration at Karunya Sugalaya. She brings exceptional international expertise to the clinic, having completed specialized training at the renowned Steno Diabetes Centre in Denmark, a global leader in diabetes care and research.

Her clinical passion lies in women's health, specifically focusing on the complex intersections of metabolic disorders and reproductive health. She is a regional expert in managing Gestational Diabetes and Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS), providing nuanced, specialized care that empowers women through critical stages of their lives.

Dr. Lakshmi is also the driving force behind the RITAM 360 programme, a holistic patient care initiative designed to provide comprehensive, wrap-around support for individuals living with chronic metabolic conditions. Her leadership ensures that the clinic operates with both administrative efficiency and an unwavering commitment to patient-centric care.
